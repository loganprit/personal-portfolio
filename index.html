About Logan

While pursuing chemical engineering at the University of Louisiana at Lafayette, I was dragging through every class I took. My junior year, I took a class about creating programs with graphical interfaces in VBA for Excel. After that moment, I realized what I wanted to do. I then began to research how I could be a software engineer without doing another 4 years of school.

That's where I found Thinkful. I changed course and now have begun my journey to becoming a software developer and couldn't be more excited!
